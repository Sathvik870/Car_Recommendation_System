The Ford Focus is a versatile hatchback that offers a balance of performance and efficiency, with its turbocharged 1.0L EcoBoost engine providing up to 123 horsepower. The Focus is equipped with a comfortable interior that features soft-touch materials, a SYNC 3 infotainment system with an 8-inch touchscreen, and smartphone connectivity. It also includes Ford’s Co-Pilot360 suite of safety features, such as lane-keeping assist, automatic high beams, and adaptive cruise control. With a fold-flat rear seat, the Focus provides 44.8 cubic feet of cargo space, making it a practical choice for city dwellers. It’s known for its sharp handling, making it a fun-to-drive option in its class.
